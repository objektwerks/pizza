Poolish Bread Recipe
--------------------
>Basic poolish bread recipe: [Bread Calculator Result](https://www.breadfriend.com/?totalWeight=900&salt=1&prefermentType=%27poolish%27&poolishPercentage=30)

Poolish Ingredients
-------------------
1. 158 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees, @ 100% hydration.
2. 1 gram yeast ( 1/4 teaspoon ).
3. 158 grams bread flour.

Poolish
-------
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in flour.

Poolish Fermentation
--------------------
1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place poolish in coverable container - then cover.
3. Ferment 12 to 24 hours @ room temperature.

Dough Ingredients
-----------------
1. 234 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 70% hydration ).
2. 1 gram yeast ( 1/4 teaspoon ).
3. 410 grams bread flour.
4. 3 grams sea salt ( 1 teaspoon ).

Dough
-----
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in poolish.
4. Mix in flour.
5. Mix in sea salt.
6. Mix in spritz of olive oil.
7. Knead dough into ball.
8. Remove dough ball from bowl and place on board - knead into smooth ball, using flour as required.

Dough Fermentation
------------------
1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place dough ball in coverable container - then cover.
2. Ferment dough ball for 2+ hours @ room temperature.
>Optionally, ferment dough ball overnight in frig.

Shape
-----
1. Spread flour on board.
2. Place dough ball on board - sprinkle flour on dough ball.
3. Shape dough into desired shape.
4. Place dough on parchment paper or silicone bread sling.
5. Score dough.

Oven
----
1. Place Dutch oven in oven.
2. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.

Bake
----
1. Remove Dutch oven from oven.
2. Place bread, contained in parchment paper or silicone bread sling, in Dutch oven.
3. Place lid on Dutch oven.
4. Place Dutch oven in oven.
5. Set timer @ 30 minutes.
6. Remove lid from Dutch oven.
7. Set timer @ 10 minutes.
8. Remove Dutch oven from oven.
9. Remove bread, contained in parchment paper, from Dutch oven and place on cooling rack.
10. Cool bread for 30+ minutes.
