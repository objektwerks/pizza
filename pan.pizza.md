Pan Pizza Recipe
----------------
>Basic 15" x 10" baking pan pizza recipe.

Ingredients
-----------
1. 280 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 1 cup + 3 tablespoons ) ( 70% hydration ).
2. 3 grams yeast ( 1 teaspoon ).
3. 400 grams bread flour ( 3 1/3 cups ).
4. 3 grams sea salt ( 1 teaspoon ).

Toppings
--------
1. Tomato sauce.
2. Shredded mozarella.
>Alter toppings as desired.

Dough
-----
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in flour.
4. Mix in sea salt.
5. Mix in spritz of olive oil.
6. No kneading required!

Fermentation
------------
1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place dough ball in coverable container - then cover.
2. Ferment dough for 4+ hours @ room temperature.
>Optionally, ferment dough overnight in frig.

Shape
-----
1. Spread olive oil in baking pan.
2. Place dough in baking pan.
3. Stretch dough evenly thoughout baking pan.
4. Cover baking pan for 1 hour.
5. Stretch dough as required.

Oven
----
1. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.

Par Bake
--------
1. Place baking pan in oven, at lowest level.
2. Bake @ 5+ minutes - to create a firm but not browned crust.
3. Remove baking pan from oven.

Build
-----
1. Add toppings and seasonings.

Bake
----
1. Place baking pan in oven.
2. Bake @ 10 minutes.
3. Inspect pizza intermittingly.
4. Remove baking pan from oven.
