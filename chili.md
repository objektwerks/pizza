Chili Recipe
------------
>Basic chili recipe.

Ingredients
-----------
1. 1 pound organic ground beef ( optional +1 pound ).
2. 1 onion.
3. 1 green pepper.
4. 1 6 ounce can of tomato paste.
5. 3 large cans of chili beans.
6. 2+ tablesppons of chipolte chili powder.
7. 1 12 oz bottle of Dogfish Head 60" or 90" IPA.

Preparation
-----------
1. Dice 1 onion.
2. Dice 1 green pepper.
3. Unwrap ground beef.
4. Open tomato paste can.
5. Open chili bean cans.
6. Drain chili beans.

Beef
----
1. Heat stove burner @ medium.
2. Place 10" skillet on stove burner.
3. Add olive oil to skillet
4. Place diced onion and pepper in the skillet and brown.
5. Place ground beef in the skillet and brown.
6. Drain liquid from skillet.

Chili
-----
1. Heat stove burner @ medium.
2. Place 6+ quart cooking pot on stove burner.
3. Add olive oil to pot.
4. Mix in beef, onion and green pepper.
5. Mix in chili beans.
6. Mix in tomato paste.
7. Mix in Dogfish Head IPA.
8. Mix in 2+ tablespoons of chipolte chili powder.
9. Stir chili every few minutes for 1+ hours.
>Don't forget to frequently taste test the chili!
