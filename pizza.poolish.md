Poolish Pizza
-------------
>Basic 12" poolish pizza recipe. [Another Take](https://www.breadfriend.com/?totalWeight=250&hydration=60&salt=1&prefermentType=%27poolish%27&poolishPercentage=30)

Poolish Ingredients
-------------------
1. 100 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees, @ 100% hydration.
2. 1 gram yeast ( 1/4 teaspoon ).
3. 100 grams 00 pizza flour.

Poolish
-------
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in flour.

Poolish Fermentation
--------------------
1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place poolish in coverable container - then cover.
3. Ferment 2 hours @ room temperature.
4. Ferment 12 to 24 hours in frig.

Dough Ingredients
-----------------
1. 21 grams cold water, @ 30% hydration.
2. 3 grams yeast ( 1 teaspoon ).
3. 70 grams 00 pizza flour.
4. 3 grams sea salt ( 1 teaspoon ).

Dough
-----
1. Pour water into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in poolish.
4. Mix in flour.
5. Mix in sea salt.
6. Mix in spritz of olive oil.
7. Knead dough into rough ball.
8. Remove dough ball from bowl and place on board.
9. Knead dough ball into smooth ball, using flour as required.

Dough Fermentation
------------------
1. Add olive oil to bottom of coverable container.
2. Place dough ball in coverable container - then cover.
3. Ferment dough ball for 2+ hours @ room temperature.

Shape
-----
1. Place dough ball on board.
2. Sprinkle flour on dough ball.
3. Shape dough into 12” circle.

Oven
----
1. Place pizza stone, steel or cast iron grill in oven, at lowest level.
2. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.

Toppings
--------
1. Tomato sauce.
2. Shredded mozzarella.
>Alter toppings as desired.

Build
-----
1. Spread semolina flour on pizza peel.
2. Place shaped dough on pizza peel.
3. Add toppings and seasonings.

Bake
----
1. Slide pizza pizza onto stone, steel or cast iron grill in oven.
2. Bake @ 10 minutes.
3. Inspect and adjust pizza intermittingly.
4. Remove pizza from oven.
