Artisan Bread Recipes
---------------------
>Artisan bread recipes - basic and sourdough.

Ingredients
-----------
1. 360 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 1 1/2 cups ) ( 80% hydration ).
2. 3 grams yeast ( 1 teaspoon ).
3. 450 grams bread flour ( 3 1/2 cups ).
4. 7 grams sea salt ( 2 teaspoons ).

Dough
-----
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in flour.
4. Mix in sea salt.
5. No kneading required!

Fermentation
------------
1. Ferment dough for 4 - 6 hours @ room temperature in mixing bowl.
>Optionally ferment dough overnight.

Shape
-----
1. Spread flour on board.
2. Place dough ball on board - sprinkle flour on dough ball.
3. Shape dough into desired shape.
4. Place dough on parchment paper.
5. Score dough.

Oven
----
1. Place Dutch oven in oven.
2. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.

Bake
----
1. Remove Dutch oven from oven.
2. Place bread, contained in parchment paper, in Dutch oven.
3. Place lid on Dutch oven.
4. Place Dutch oven in oven.
5. Set timer @ 30 minutes.
6. Remove lid from Dutch oven.
7. Set timer @ 10 minutes.
8. Remove Dutch oven from oven.
9. Remove bread, contained in parchment paper, from Dutch oven and place on cooling rack.
10. Cool bread for 30+ minutes.
