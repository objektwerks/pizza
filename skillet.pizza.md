Skillet Pizza Recipe
--------------------
>Basic 12" cast iron skillet pizza recipe.

Ingredients
-----------
1. 320 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 1 1/3 cups ) ( 80% hydration ).
2. 3 grams yeast ( 1 teaspoon ).
3. 400 grams bread flour ( 3 1/4 cups ).
4. 7 grams sea salt ( 2 teaspoons ).

Toppings
--------
1. Tomato sauce.
2. Shredded mozarella.
>Alter toppings as desired.

Dough
-----
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in flour.
4. Mix in sea salt.
5. No kneading required!

Fermentation
------------
1. Cover bowl.
2. Ferment dough for 4-6 hours @ room temperature.
>Optionally ferment dough overnight.

Shape
-----
1. Spread olive oil in skillet.
2. Place dough in skillet.
3. Stretch dough evenly thoughout skillet.
4. Cover and let dough rest for 1 hour.

Par Bake
--------
1. Place skillet in oven, at lowest level.
2. Bake @ 3+ minutes, until bottom crust is browning.
3. Remove skillet from oven.

Oven
----
1. Pre-heat oven @ 500 degrees.

Build
-----
1. Add toppings and seasonings.

Bake
----
1. Place skillet in oven.
2. Bake @ 12+ minutes.
3. Inspect pizza intermittingly.
4. Remove skillet from oven.
