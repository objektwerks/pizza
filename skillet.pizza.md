Skillet Pizza Recipe
--------------------
>Basic 12" cast iron skillet pizza recipe.

Ingredients
-----------
1. 240 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 1 cup ) ( 80% hydration ).
2. 3 grams yeast ( 1 teaspoon ).
3. 300 grams bread flour ( 2 1/2 cups ).
4. 3 grams sea salt ( 1 teaspoon ).

Toppings
--------
1. Tomato sauce.
2. Shredded mozarella.
>Alter toppings as desired.

Dough
-----
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in flour.
4. Mix in sea salt.
5. Mix in spritz of olive oil.
6. No kneading required!

Fermentation
------------
1. Cover bowl.
2. Ferment dough for 4+ hours @ room temperature.

Shape
-----
1. Spread olive oil in skillet.
2. Place dough in skillet.
3. Stretch dough evenly thoughout skillet.
4. Cover baking pan for 1 hour.
5. Stretch dough as required.

Oven
----
1. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.

Build
-----
1. Add toppings and seasonings.

Bake
----
1. Place skillet in oven.
2. Bake @ 15 minutes.
3. Inspect pizza intermittingly.
4. Remove skillet from oven.
5. Let pizza rest in skillet for 5 minutes.

Crust
-----
>If the crust is not brown and crispy:
1. Heat stove top burner @ medium heat.
2. Place skillet on burner until crust is brown and crispy.
