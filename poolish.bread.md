Poolish Bread Recipe
--------------------
>Basic poolish bread recipe.

Poolish Ingredients
-------------------
1. 100 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 1/2 cup ) ( 100% hydration ).
2. 1 gram yeast ( 1/3 teaspoon ).
3. 100 grams bread flour ( 3/4 cup ).
>Yielding 200 grams of poolish. ( 50% of the dough )

Dough Ingredients
-----------------
1. 200 grams water @ 100F / 37C degrees ( 5/6 cup ) ( 50% hydration ).
2. 3 grams yeast ( 1 teaspoon ).
3. 200 grams poolish ( 3/4 cup )
4. 400 grams bread flour ( 3 1/3 cups ).
5. 3 grams sea salt ( 1 teaspoon ).

Dough
-----
1. Pour water @ 100F / 37C degrees into a bowl.
2. Mix in yeast.
3. Mix in poolish.
4. Mix in flour.
5. Mix in sea salt.
6. Mix in spritz of olive oil.
7. Knead dough into ball.
8. Remove dough ball from bowl and place on board - knead into smooth ball, using flour as required.
