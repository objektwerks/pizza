Kimchi Pancake
--------------
>Basic kimchi pancake recipe.

Ingredients
-----------
1. Flour ( 100 grams )
2. Water ( 75 grams )
3. Kimchi ( 200 grams )
4. Kimchi Liquid ( 3+ tablespoons, sourced from kimchi container )
5. Gochujang ( 1 tablespoon )
6. Japanese Roasted Sesame Oil ( 1 tablespoon )
>Adjust ingredients to taste.

Batter
------
1. Mix in flour.
2. Mix in water.
3. Mix in kimchi liquid.
4. Mix in gochujang.
5. Mix in roasted sesame oil.

Oven
----
1. Place 8 inch bakinng pan or alternative in oven.
2. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.
>A cast iron skillet or grill works well.

Bake
----
1. Remove baking pan or alternative from oven.
2. Oil baking pan.
3. Spread kimchi pancake batter evenly across baking pan.
4. Place baking pan in oven.
5. Bake @ 20 minutes.
6. Inspect kimchi pancake intermittingly.
7. Remove baking pan from oven and slice.
