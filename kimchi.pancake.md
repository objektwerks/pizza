Kimchi Pancake Recipe
---------------------
>Basic kimchi pancake recipe.

Ingredients
-----------
1. 100 grams flour.
2. 70 grams water.
3. 200 grams kimchi.
4. 3+ tablespoons of kimchi liquid.
5. 1 tablespoon gochujang.
6. 1 tablespoon roasted sesame oil.
7. 2 scallions
>Adjust ingredients to taste and size.

Batter
------
1. Mix in flour.
2. Mix in water.
3. Mix in kimchi liquid.
4. Mix in gochujang.
5. Mix in roasted sesame oil.
6. Cut and mix in scallions.

Oven
----
1. Place 8+ inch bakinng pan in oven.
2. Pre-heat oven @ 450 degrees.
>A cast iron skillet is a great alternative.

Bake
----
1. Remove baking pan from oven.
2. Spread kimchi pancake batter evenly across baking pan.
3. Place baking pan in oven.
4. Bake @ 20 minutes.
5. Inspect kimchi pancake intermittingly.
6. Remove baking pan from oven.
>Alternatively, a skillet and stove can be used.
